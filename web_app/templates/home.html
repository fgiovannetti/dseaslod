About LIFT

Linked data from TEI (short: LIFT) is a Python-based toolkit for extracting RDF triples from TEI documents designed to support the creation of semantic digital scholarly editions. The toolkit is particularly useful to those taking their first steps towards the use of knowledge graphs to enrich TEI-based editions.

LIFT lets you:

  • select what information to extract from your TEI document: you can choose among different extraction options including people, relationships, events, places, bibliographic references, critical apparatus;
  • choose a serialization: you can view and download your RDF graph using XML, Notation3 or N-triples;
  • customize all extraction scripts: all scripts can be freely downloaded; a step-by-step guide available as a manipulable Jupyter notebook will help you write your own customization.

LIFT comes with a detailed documentation suggesting further readings and useful resources.
